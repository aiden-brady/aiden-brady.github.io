One Less Colour (Ongoing)


One Less Colour is a bi-monthly, hour-long radio show on Dublin Digital Radio. It combines field recordings,
spoken word and selected music to consider new narrative forms for the radio context. As an experimental
performance it seeks novel ways to combine sonic elements to create cohesive narrative structures.

You can browse my resident's page on DDR's website to find previous episodes of the show.
