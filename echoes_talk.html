"Echoes talk "




"Echoes Talk" is an interactive work which uses spatialised audio to explore the relationship between
superficial or 'surface level' conversation and the unsaid, authentic emotion which may lie beneath it.

Using a black box inlaid with two infrared sensors as a focal point, four speakers are placed around
the room, only partially visible. At intervals, the audio moves around the room from speaker to speaker.
This operates on a track by track basis, meaning that as several tracks play at once during the work,
many speakers are audible throughout as well.

As the audience member approaches the box, snippets of either more vulnerable or encouraging speech
are heard and become mixed in amongst the 'idle' chatter. Whether the audio is vulnerable or encouraging,
depends which side of the box is approached.

The aim, in intermingling the profound speech with the inane and hiding the speakers among everyday items,
was to consider the relationship between protection and concealment, or alternatively, suppression and revelation.
